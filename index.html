The Cheat Code

Tailoring Under $50

Tailoring is the single most underused cheat code in women's fashion. These alterations cost less than a new garment — and make everything look custom.

$10 — $15

Hem Pants

The #1 most impactful alteration. Clean hems transform any pair of pants from sloppy to polished.

$15 — $25

Taper the Leg

Slim the leg from knee to ankle for a modern, streamlined silhouette without buying new pants.

$15 — $20

Shorten Sleeves

Too-long sleeves make even expensive blazers look borrowed. A quick fix with massive impact.

$15 — $30

Add Darts

Darts create shape where there is none. Transform a boxy blouse into a fitted masterpiece.

$15 — $25

Take in the Waist

The waistband fix for jeans, skirts, and trousers. One of the most requested alterations.

$5 — $10

Move Buttons

A tiny shift in button placement can eliminate gaping and improve the entire fit of a garment.
